Battle of the Rednotes

Rednotes are 20-30 second audio clips that can be attached to text messages. Sort of like emojis or photos, but for music. Rednotes are a great tool for artists and their fans to spread their music.

The Battle of the Rednotes Contestwill award the artist or band whose Rednote is shared the ost Rednote is holding a contest. The winner will be the artist or band whose Rednote is shared the most.

The winner will receive an iPad Mini and a Blue Microphone plus a ton of free promotion.

The sooner you enter, the better your chance to win, but to be considered, you must enter by February 1, 2014.
