Battle of the Rednotes Contest

A Rednote™ is a 20-30 second audio clip that can be attached to a text message. Sort of like an emoji or photo, but for music. A Rednote is a clear emotion that can be conveyed by your song (ex: "Tonight's gonna be a good night.") It is a great tool for artists to spread their music with their fans.

The Battle of the Rednotes Contest will award an iPad Mini and Blue Microphone to the artist or band whose Rednote is shared the most.
